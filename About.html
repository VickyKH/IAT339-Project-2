About us

Our Story

Dirty Mouth Donuts is an ironically cute donut shop with an unapologetic edge; we’re naughty but nice. We're the coolest kid on the block. We sport the latest fashions and know where all the cool kids hang out. Everybody wants to be our friends. We look sweet and innocent on the outside, but once you get to know them, you will be flip-flopped by our kawaii-punked edge.

So with our terrible attitude but great taste, we sought out to make the coolest donut shop ever. We specialize in novelty flavored donuts such as pickle, vanilla-soy-sauce and marshmallow-popcorn. We're still open so people must love us. Come on in and get a Dirty Mouth.

Our Values

We strive on great fashion, punk culture, Harajuku culture and bad attitude. And you should too. We didn't grow up in the nicest neighborhoods. Our parents are also are heros. They gave up so much to give us the kind of life that we're able to live now. With this embedded determination, we wanted to create something that will honor our upbringing and unwillingnest to give in to all of the terrible things that happens in this world. Though very unrefine, you'll fine that these novelty donuts are made with finest and thoughtfulness. So eventhough we look like we're gonna rip you apart, we're very welcoming and caring people. So again, please come on in and show us your Dirty Mouth.
